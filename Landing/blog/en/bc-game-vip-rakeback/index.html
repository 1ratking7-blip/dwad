BC.Game VIP and Rakeback — How the Loyalty Program Works

Beyond the welcome bonus and the daily Lucky Spin, BC.Game has an accumulating loyalty program — a VIP club with rakeback. Unlike a one-time welcome bonus, this is what actually matters for players who stick around on the platform long-term, not just for the first few days after signing up.

What rakeback is, and why it's not the same as a bonus

Rakeback is a partial return based on your betting volume (not your winnings, and not your deposit) — BC.Game's loyalty program offers rakeback up to 20%. Unlike a welcome bonus, which is tied to wagering requirements on a specific amount, rakeback accrues continuously as you play — it's effectively a reduction in the effective house edge for active players, not a one-time sign-up incentive.

Cashback — a separate mechanic

Beyond rakeback, the platform also has weekly and monthly cashback — a periodic return of a portion of your net losses over the billing period. This doesn't replace rakeback, it complements it: rakeback is calculated from betting volume continuously, cashback is calculated at the end of each week/month. Exact percentages and terms depend on your VIP tier and change periodically on the platform's side — check the VIP section on BC.Game itself for current figures; we deliberately don't duplicate numbers here that could go stale faster than this page gets updated.

How to get into the program

There's no separate application form needed — VIP status on most crypto casinos, including BC.Game, grows automatically based on your account's betting volume. The higher the tier, the higher the rakeback and the access to additional perks (a personal account manager, faster withdrawals, exclusive promos — the standard set for VIP programs in this space). The easiest way to activate your account is through the ZHELEZO referral link — the same registration mechanism used for the welcome bonus.
